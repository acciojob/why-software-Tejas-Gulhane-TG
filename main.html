Why I want to become a developer?

Passion for technology: Many aspiring developers have a genuine interest in technology and enjoy working with computers, software, and programming languages. They find the process of building and creating digital solutions exciting and fulfilling. Problem-solving: Developers are like problem solvers who enjoy tackling complex challenges. They possess a logical and analytical mindset, constantly seeking innovative solutions to various problems. The satisfaction of overcoming obstacles and finding efficient solutions can be a major driving force.
